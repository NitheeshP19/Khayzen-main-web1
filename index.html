Technical Overview

This motor is a **brushless, permanent magnet synchronous motor** with a multi-layered **printed circuit board** embedded with copper-etched conductors in a stator, that works in conjunction with permanent magnets in the rotor with **Axial Flux and Air gap**.

The state-of-the-art motor technology is patented. **High-tech edge computing** makes use of printed circuit board manufacturing to print stators for **coreless axial flux machines**. This approach fundamentally changes how motors are designed and manufactured.
