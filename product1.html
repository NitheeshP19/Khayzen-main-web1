Axial Motor

High-Performance Industrial Motor Technology

This motor is a brushless, permanent magnet synchronous motor with a multi-layered printed circuit board embedded with copper-etched conductors in a stator, that works in conjunction with permanent magnets in the rotor with Axial Flux and Air gap. The state-of-the-art motor technology is patented. High-tech edge computing makes use of printed circuit board manufacturing to print stators for coreless axial flux machines. This approach fundamentally changes how motors are designed and manufacturedrial applications. Designed with precision engineering and advanced materials, these motors provide reliable performance with minimal maintenance requirements. The compact design makes them ideal for space-constrained applications while maintaining exceptional power output.
